☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes retail coffee shop sales data to extract real business insights that directly influence revenue, customer behavior, product strategy, and store-level performance.

The goal of this analysis is not just visualization — it is to identify where revenue is generated, where it is lost, and how data-backed decisions can improve overall business performance.

🎯 Business Objectives
Identify peak sales windows
Detect best-selling and high-profit products
Compare store-wise performance
Understand customer buying behavior
Optimize product size strategy
Support revenue growth decisions
📊 KPIs Tracked
Total Sales
Footfall
Average Bill Per Person
Average Orders Per Person
Revenue by Product & Category
Revenue by Store Location
Hourly, Daily & Monthly Sales Trends
🧠 Key Business Insights
🔥 Peak Revenue Window (7 AM – 10 AM)
Sales spike heavily in the morning, with the highest volume around 9–10 AM.

🍵 Tea = #1 Revenue Category (35%)
Tea contributes the highest share of revenue compared to other categories.

🥤 Large & Regular Cups Drive 61% of Orders
Large and Regular sizes together account for more than half of all orders.

🏬 Hell’s Kitchen = Highest Converting Store
Hell’s Kitchen generates the highest revenue with comparatively lower footfall.

💰 Top Revenue Products
Barista Espresso, Brewed Chai Tea, Hot Chocolate generate the majority of revenue.

📅 Busiest Days = Monday & Tuesday
Order volumes are highest on Monday and Tuesday.
